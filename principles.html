Our Principles

Herely is built by a professor for educators who value clarity, simplicity, and student and participant trust. It is designed to respect participants, reduce friction, and make attendance tracking ethical by default.

1. No Accounts Required

Participants never need to register, download apps, or remember passwords.

2. Zero Data Retention

We store information only to track attendance, and by default all course data is hard deleted once a program is complete.

3. You Control the Data

Delete any course, roster, or attendance record at any time.

4. No Surveillance

There are no hidden analytics or cookies, and your data never leaves your control.

5. Privacy First

We don’t sell data. We don’t run ads.
